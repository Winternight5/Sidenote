What is Sidenote?

Sidenote is a note-taking app, a todo list app, and a drawing app all in one. Though simple, the application's main features were designed to be extremely powerful. Enabling users to easily append tasks onto various to-do lists, or even take notes with the liberty of typing or writing (if on a tablet/mobile device), Sidenote makes managing your tasks a breeze. It even includes the ability to share notes/todo lists among peers via their email addresses. Upon registration, users will be able to immediately begin tracking their daily tasks, taking miscellaneous notes, and sharing them with colleagues throughout the day. With our sleek, easy-to-navigate user interface, Sidenote makes organizing your life a fun, elegant experience.

Sidenote runs on a Python-Flask server-side program that retrieves data from a PostgreSQL database to keep track of user data. We utilized standard tools such as Git as a version control system and Travis CI as a continuous integration service. Real time editing of notes/todo lists was made possible through the integration of websockets.

Sidenote was built with technologies such as: Python, Flask, JavaScript, AJAX, Socket.io, HTML, CSS/SCSS, Materialize.css, Travis CI, and Heroku.
